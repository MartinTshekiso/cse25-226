About DTEF

The Department of Tertiary Education Financing (DTEF) is a government institution dedicated to providing financial support to deserving Botswana citizens pursuing tertiary education both locally and internationally.

Our mission is to make quality education accessible to all qualified students regardless of their financial background, thereby contributing to national development and economic growth.

Through our modernized online platform, we have significantly reduced application turnaround times and improved accessibility for students across the country.
