About PawConnect Botswana

Connecting loving homes with wonderful dogs

Our Story

PawConnect Botswana was founded in 2019 with a simple but powerful mission: to connect loving families with dogs in need of homes. We noticed a gap in comprehensive pet care services in Botswana and set out to fill it.

What started as a small rescue operation in Gaborone has grown into a full-service pet care center. Today, we serve hundreds of dogs and families across Botswana, providing adoption services, veterinary care, grooming, training, and much more.

Every dog deserves a loving home, and every family deserves the right match. We believe in the power of human-animal bonds and work tirelessly to make them happen.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs while providing exceptional pet care services to the Botswana community.

Compassion

We treat every dog with love and care. Each animal is valued and respected as an individual with unique needs.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in all services - adoption, veterinary care, grooming, and training.

Responsibility

We are responsible for the welfare of our dogs and committed to supporting adoptive families for life.
