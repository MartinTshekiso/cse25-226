About DTEF

Understanding Our Mission and Vision

Who We Are

The Department of Tertiary Education Financing (DTEF) is a government institution established to provide financial support to deserving Botswana citizens pursuing tertiary education both locally and internationally.

We operate under the Ministry of Education and are committed to ensuring that financial constraints do not prevent qualified students from accessing quality higher education.

Our organization has evolved from a manual, centralized system based in Gaborone to a modern, accessible online platform serving students across the entire country.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide equitable access to quality tertiary education financing for deserving Botswana citizens, thereby contributing to national development, economic growth, and the creation of a skilled workforce.

Our Vision

To be a world-class education financing institution that empowers Botswana's youth through accessible, efficient, and transparent sponsorship programs that transform lives and build a prosperous nation.

Our Core Values

Equity & Fairness

We ensure equal access to sponsorship opportunities for all qualified students regardless of background or location.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct in all our operations and decision-making.

Efficiency

We continuously improve our processes to reduce turnaround times and enhance service delivery.

Innovation

We embrace technology and modern solutions to better serve our students and stakeholders.

Key Achievements

Digital Transformation

Successfully transitioned from manual, centralized system to a modern online platform accessible nationwide.

Reduced Processing Time

Decreased application turnaround time from 3-4 months to an average of 14 days.

Increased Accessibility

Extended sponsorship opportunities to students across all regions of Botswana with equal access.

Enhanced Transparency

Implemented real-time tracking and notification systems for improved communication.

Increased Funding

Supported over 50,000 students with more than P2.5 billion in sponsorship funding.

High Satisfaction

Achieved 95% satisfaction rate among applicants and beneficiaries.

Our Sponsorship Programs

Undergraduate Sponsorship

Financial support for students pursuing first-degree programs at recognized tertiary institutions locally and internationally.

Postgraduate Sponsorship

Support for students pursuing master's and doctoral degrees in priority fields for national development.

Technical & Vocational

Sponsorship for students pursuing technical and vocational qualifications in high-demand sectors.

International Programs

Support for students pursuing education at international institutions in priority fields.

Special Needs Support

Additional support for students with disabilities and special circumstances.

Merit-Based Awards

Recognition and support for high-achieving students pursuing strategic fields.
