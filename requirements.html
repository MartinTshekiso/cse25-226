Sponsorship Requirements

Eligibility criteria and documentation needed

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for DTEF sponsorship, you must meet all of the following criteria:

Botswana Citizenship

You must be a citizen of Botswana. Proof of citizenship will be required.

Secondary Education Completion

You must have completed secondary education or equivalent qualification (BGCSE, IGCSE, or equivalent).

Tertiary Institution Admission

You must have been accepted to a recognized tertiary institution (university, college, or technical school) in Botswana or internationally.

Financial Need

You must demonstrate financial need. Annual family income should generally not exceed P150,000 for undergraduate programs.

Academic Performance

You must have achieved minimum academic grades (typically Grade C or equivalent) and maintain satisfactory academic progress.

No Duplicate Sponsorship

You must not be receiving similar sponsorship from another government or private source for the same program.

Good Standing

You must have no outstanding legal issues or criminal convictions that would disqualify you from receiving government sponsorship.

Required Documents

You must submit the following documents with your application:

National ID Copy

A clear photocopy or digital scan of your valid national ID. File size: Max 5MB. Format: PDF, JPG, or PNG.

Academic Records

Official transcripts or certificates from your secondary school and any previous tertiary education. Must be certified copies.

Proof of Admission

Official admission letter from your tertiary institution showing your program, duration, and start date.

Income Verification

Recent payslip (last 3 months), tax return, or letter from employer confirming annual income. For self-employed: business registration and financial statements.

Birth Certificate

Certified copy of your birth certificate to verify citizenship and age.

Bank Account Details

Copy of your bank account statement or passbook showing your name and account number for fund disbursement.

Additional Supporting Documents

Any other documents that support your application, such as disability certificate, letter of recommendation, or evidence of special circumstances.

Program-Specific Requirements

  • Minimum Grade C in BGCSE or equivalent
  • Annual family income not exceeding P150,000
  • Full-time enrollment required
  • Minimum 2-year program duration
  • Maintain minimum GPA of 2.0 annually

  • Bachelor's degree from recognized institution
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 in undergraduate studies
  • Program must be in priority field for national development
  • Full-time enrollment required
  • Annual family income not exceeding P200,000

  • Minimum Grade D in BGCSE or equivalent
  • Program must be in high-demand sector
  • Annual family income not exceeding P120,000
  • Full-time enrollment required
  • Maintain satisfactory progress in practical and theoretical components
