Support & FAQ

Find answers to common questions

To be eligible for DTEF sponsorship, you must:

  • Be a Botswana citizen
  • Have completed secondary education or equivalent
  • Have been accepted to a recognized tertiary institution
  • Demonstrate financial need
  • Have a minimum academic grade requirement
  • Not be receiving similar sponsorship from another source

Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Create an account on the DTEF portal
  2. Complete the application form with your personal and educational information
  3. Upload required documents (ID, academic records, proof of admission, income verification)
  4. Review your application for accuracy
  5. Submit your application
  6. You will receive a confirmation email with your application ID

Required documents include:

  • Copy of National ID
  • Academic records (transcripts/certificates)
  • Proof of admission from your institution
  • Income verification document (payslip, tax return, or letter from employer)
  • Any additional supporting documents as requested

All documents must be in PDF, JPG, or PNG format and not exceed 5MB each.

Most applications are processed within 14 days. However, processing time may vary depending on:

  • Completeness of your application
  • Quality and clarity of submitted documents
  • Volume of applications received
  • Need for additional information or verification

You can track your application status in real-time through your dashboard.

You will receive notifications through multiple channels:

  • Email: Official decision letters and important updates
  • SMS: Status updates and reminders
  • Dashboard: Real-time status tracking in your account
  • Phone: In case of urgent matters

Make sure to keep your contact information up to date.

The sponsorship amount depends on several factors:

  • Your financial need assessment
  • Cost of your program
  • Your academic performance
  • Available funding
  • Type of program (undergraduate, postgraduate, etc.)

Sponsorship typically covers tuition fees and may include living allowances. The exact amount will be communicated in your approval letter.

Funding disbursement typically occurs:

  • After your application is approved
  • Upon confirmation of your enrollment
  • Within 5-7 business days of approval
  • Funds are transferred directly to your institution or bank account

You will receive confirmation of the transfer through email and SMS.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password
  6. Log in with your new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Yes, the DTEF portal uses industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure password storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Compliance with government data protection standards
  • Two-factor authentication for sensitive operations

Your personal and financial information is protected and confidential.

Yes, you can submit applications for multiple programs. However:

  • Each application must be complete and submitted separately
  • You can only receive sponsorship for one program at a time
  • If approved for multiple programs, you must choose which one to pursue
  • Sponsorship is not transferable between programs

If your application is rejected:

  • You will receive a detailed letter explaining the reasons
  • You can request a review of the decision
  • You may be able to reapply in the next application cycle
  • Contact support for guidance on improving your application

Don't hesitate to reach out if you need clarification on the decision.
