The adoption process typically takes 2-3 weeks from application submission. This includes application review, home visit (if applicable), and final approval. We want to ensure the best match for both you and your new furry friend!

Adoption fees range from P300-P800 depending on the dog's age and health status. The fee includes vaccinations, microchipping, and an initial vet checkup. All funds go directly to caring for our dogs.
